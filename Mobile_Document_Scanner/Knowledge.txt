01: np.diff 后面一个元素减去前面一个元素
import numpy as np
a=np.array([1, 6, 7, 8, 12])
diff_x1 = np.diff(a)
print("diff_x1",diff_x1)
# diff_x1 [5 1 1 4]
# [6-1,7-6,8-7,12-8]
